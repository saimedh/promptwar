      How It Works
      
        
      Three steps from prompt to leaderboard-ready score.
      
        
      
          
      
            
      01
      
            
      🔑
      
            
      SHA-256 Cache Lookup
      
            
      Your prompt + task + rubric are hashed to 24 hex chars. Memorystore Redis is checked first — cache hits return in <1ms.
      
          
      
          
      
            
      02
      
            
      🤖
      
            
      Gemini Scores 5 Dimensions
      
            
      On a cache miss, Vertex AI Gemini 1.5 Flash evaluates Clarity, Specificity, Task Alignment, Output Format, and Conciseness at temperature 0.1.
      
          
      
          
      
            
      03
      
            
      📊
      
            
      Score → Leaderboard
      
            
      overall_score = mean(scores) × 10. Results are cached with TTL and published to Pub/Sub for the live leaderboard.
